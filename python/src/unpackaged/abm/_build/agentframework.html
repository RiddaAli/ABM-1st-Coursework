share_with_neighbours(neighbourhood)[source]¶

Calculating the distance to each of the other agent, and if they tend to be within the neighbourhood distance then sets its and its neighbours stores equal to the average of their two stores

Looping through the agents in self.agents. 1) Calculating the distance between self and the current other agent. 2) If the distance is less than or equal to neighbourhood

(by default 20 is the radius around agents)then add self.store and agent.store.

  1. Calculate the average by dividing the sum by 2.

  2. Store this average into both the self.store and agent.store.
